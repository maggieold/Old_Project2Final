Brenen's Coffee Cafe

Brenen's Coffee Cafe, located at 38 South Court Street, is owned and operated by OU alumni. It's been a tradition to many since 1991, operated by Josh and Jessica Thomas since 2000. One special highlight of Brenen's is that they have their very own meal plan! You can choose between a number of meals you want to purchase, and use them throughout the entirety of the year. They never expire, and you can always refill them! Brenen's serves breakfast and lunch, so it's a great stop any time of the day.

Donkey Coffee and Espresso

Donkey Coffee is a staple in Athens, OH located at 17 W Washington St. It gives into the town's local, unqiue identity. With a backroom filled with artwork, a stage for all kinds of performaces, and events for the community, you'll never find a dull moment at Donkey Coffee. With more than 4,000 square feet of cozy space to enjoy the great coffee, their fundamental mission is to love the world through fair and just business practices. They welcome everyone and say, "Donkey is a place where you belong."

Ornery Vet's Coffee

Ornery Vet's Cafe may be new to Athens, but they are just as high quality as the long standing coffee shops in town. Located at 30 East State Street, they have everything you would need from coffee, to tea, to snacks, boba, merch, and more. Ornery Vet's also puts on events for the community, including lemonade stands! Through their website you can make donations, buy gift cards, order food, and more. Check it out!

Court Street Coffee

Court Street Coffee opened its doors to the city of Athens in October of 2009, with the goal of providing a high quality product and friendly, community-focused culture.You can find them at 67 South Court Street, where they provide a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere, welcoming faculty, students, and local residents. This convienent location is perfect for students as they walk to class, head to the univeristy center, or just need a place to sit and relax.

Town's End Coffee

Town's End Coffee is a hidden gem in Athens. Located at 9133 Rock Riffle Rd, they hope to bring a distinct experience and flavor to your cup. Town's End has been working to be your specialty coffee pit stop since the early 90's. They pride themselves on their attention to detail, making sure your cup is the best possible. While they are a little bit off of Ohio University's Campus, it is well worth the extra walk or the drive.
